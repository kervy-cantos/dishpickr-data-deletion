DishPickr Data Deletion

Last updated: June 2026

DishPickr does not require users to create an account. Most app data, including custom dishes, meal plans, grocery items, and selected dish images, is stored locally on your device.

Delete Your Data Inside the App

You can delete your locally stored DishPickr data inside the app:

  • Open DishPickr
  • Go to Settings
  • Use options such as Clear Meal Plan, Clear Grocery Data, or Clear Custom Dishes

Delete All App Data

You can also delete all locally stored data by clearing the app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app.

Request Data Deletion

Since DishPickr currently does not use accounts or cloud sync, we do not store your recipes, meal plans, grocery items, or dish images on our servers.
